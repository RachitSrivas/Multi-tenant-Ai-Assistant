2. RETURN & REFUND POLICY
- Standard Returns: Customers may return unused, unopened products within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. 
- Defective Items: If a product is defective (e.g., our bamboo pens arrive broken), we will replace it free of charge within 14 days of delivery. No return of the broken item is necessary; we just need a photo.
- Custom/Branded Orders: Corporate gifts with custom logos engraved cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect.

3. SHIPPING INFORMATION
- Standard Eco-Shipping: Takes 5-7 business days. It uses carbon-neutral delivery partners. Free for orders over $50. Otherwise, it costs $5.99.
- Express Shipping: Takes 2-3 business days. Costs $15.99.
- International Shipping: We currently only ship to the US and Canada.

4. PRODUCT FAQ
Q: Are the biodegradable notebooks actually compostable?
A: Yes! Our notebooks use recycled stone paper and soy-based inks. You can place them in a commercial compost bin, and they will break down in 90 to 120 days.

Q: How do I refill the bamboo pen?
A: Twist the metal tip counter-clockwise. Pull out the empty ink cartridge and insert an Organeo Universal Refill cartridge. Twist the tip back on.

Q: Do you offer bulk discounts for enterprise customers?
A: Yes, we offer a 15% discount on orders of 500+ items, and a 25% discount for orders exceeding 2,000 items. Please contact sales@organeo.com for bulk invoicing.

5. SUPPORT AGENT GUIDELINES
When responding to customers, always maintain a friendly, eco-conscious, and helpful tone. If a customer is angry about a delayed shipment, apologize and offer a 10% discount code (CODE: GREEN10) for their next purchase as an apology. If you do not know the answer to a question, tell the customer you will escalate it to a human manager.
